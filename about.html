About me

I'm marine engineer. Studied at Maritime University in Sczecin / Poland & working as engineer since 1999. I've travelled the world all over, have been in both Americas, Australia, Africa, Asia & Europe naturally as I live there. Having opportunity to see all of those continents and countries within them is a huge advantage of this job.

There is also another side of this profession, being apart from family & limited contact with people. Having just max 23 companions onboard and limited access to the internet makes life difficult nowadays.

Can you imagine how CS50 knowledge might help a guy with more then 20 years experience? With latest technology, engines with electronically controlled injection, more sofisticated automatic systems, growing IT technology, it's a great to understand what's happening "under the hood".
